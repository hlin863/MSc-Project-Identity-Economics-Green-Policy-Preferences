General Concern: 75% of adults in Great Britain are worried about climate change, with 43% feeling anxious about the future environment.

Demographics: Women and younger adults are more likely to be worried and anxious about climate change than men and older adults.

Behavioral Changes: Those worried about climate change are more likely to make lifestyle changes, with 81% of adults reporting some change.

Perceptions and Attitudes: Common concerns include impacts on future generations, feelings of helplessness, and the costs associated with eco-friendly changes.
